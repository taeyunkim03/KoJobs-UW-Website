EVENTS & HIGHLIGHTS

At KOJOBS UW, we host a variety of events to help students connect, grow, and succeed. From mentoring programs to networking nights, our events are designed to empower Huskies with valuable career resources and opportunities. Explore our past events and see how we make a difference.

2024-25

Corporate Seminar

Invited a panel of UW alumni and professionals from the Greater Seattle Area, including Amazon, Google, Meta, PwC, and UW Dental School, to provide career guidance as well as practical advice and counseling. Offered opportunities for resume assistance from the professionals.

Career Session

Hosted a career workshop featuring past Boeing & NASA intern and AWS recruiter. The workshop aimed to provide students with insights into the job market and tips for securing employment. Mainly focussed on resume building and interview preparation along with LinkedIn usage guidance.

KLUE

Klue is an outdoor activity where teams, organized by major, navigate the UW campus, completing designated missions and quizzes to gather clues. The team that reaches the final destination first will be awarded the grand prize.

KO:NNECT

KO:NNECT is a mentoring program that matches pre-major underclassmen with upperclassmen in their major. Professionally organized event to assist students with academic and career guidance while allowing them to network with peer students in the same field.

General Meeting

Introduction to our student organization at the beginning of the school year. Presented our visions and goals, upcoming events, organizational structure, and officer members.
