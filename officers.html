OFFICERS & DEPARTMENTS

At KoJobs UW, each department is made up of passionate and hardworking students dedicated to connecting Huskies with valuable career opportunities. As current officers, we are committed to creating a supportive platform that helps students navigate job searches, internships, and professional growth.
Driven by a shared vision, we collaborate to bring career resources, networking events, and job opportunities to the UW community. Our dedication ensures that KoJobs UW remains a trusted space where students can take the next step toward their future. Get to know the team behind the mission.
